Remove greyed-out star ratings in iTunes Library

It occurs today: I tried to create a playlist that is based on rating. Somehow the filter seems not working, the playlist would include songs I actually did not rate. Those songs has something in common: in their rating field, stars are not "obvious". Instead, a grey color replace the normal rating color.

The problem is that the whole album is rated. To remove those "grey stars", simply show album in iTunes Library and cancel the album rating.
